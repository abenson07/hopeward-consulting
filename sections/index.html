Evermind exists at the intersection of ecology, clarity, and design

Our story

From Brussels, with intention

This is someWhat if a design system followed the same principles as ecological architecture? What if a Webflow template could feel as solid — and as invisible — as the foundations of a well-built structure?

We imagined a consultancy in Brussels, advising cities and institutions on how to build with care — following not just regulations, but deeper standards of responsibility. That idea became our blueprint. Not for buildings, but for websites.

In a world of loud brands, Evermind™ is a quiet system. It doesn’t shout. It supports. It doesn’t push aesthetics — it lets them emerge from clarity, rhythm, and intention.

Built using modern Webflow practices, Variables, modular layouts, and a full Figma file — Evermind™ is for brands that want to feel natural and credible. It’s for those who believe that good design is not about decoration, but structure. Not about trends, but timelessness.

This is our way of working: With softness, with precision, with long-term thinking — and with a little bit of Brussels stoicism. text inside of a div block.

Working with us

What It Feels Like to Move Hopeward.

Hopeward isn't just a name, it's a direction. It takes shape in grounded strategy, aligned leadership, and systems that transform complexity into clarity and conversation into coordinated, sustained action.
More templates
Is Hopeward another consultancy that delivers a polished report and walks away?

No. We're built for mission-driven leaders who need a partner when the work is heavy. We stay grounded in strategy, strengthen collaboration, and help teams sustain momentum — not just hand off a document and leave.

Who do you work with?

Nonprofit and backbone leaders, cross-sector collaboratives, and teams where multiple partners need to move together — especially when growth, complexity, or change makes the path feel unclear.

What challenges do you usually help with?

We're often brought in when priorities compete, coordination gets strained, or strategy needs to become shared action. Common focuses include clarifying direction, aligning leadership, improving how partners work together, and designing lightweight systems that keep progress moving.

How does an engagement typically work?

We start by understanding context, constraints, and relationships. Then we co-create actionable pathways — facilitation, planning, and practical supports — so decisions stay owned by your team and momentum is easier to maintain.

Do you support collaboratives and cross-sector partnerships?

Yes. We work often where sectors meet — helping groups build trust, shared language, and operating rhythms so partnerships can move from intent to coordinated execution.

How do we get started?

Usually with a conversation about what feels stuck, what outcomes matter, and what a sensible first step looks like. We'll be clear about scope, cadence, and how we'll collaborate before the work begins.

Working with us

What It Feels Like to Move Hopeward.

Hopeward isn't just a name, it's a direction. It takes shape in grounded strategy, aligned leadership, and systems that transform complexity into clarity and conversation into coordinated, sustained action.
More templates
Is Hopeward another consultancy that delivers a polished report and walks away?

No. We're built for mission-driven leaders who need a partner when the work is heavy. We stay grounded in strategy, strengthen collaboration, and help teams sustain momentum — not just hand off a document and leave.

Who do you work with?

Nonprofit and backbone leaders, cross-sector collaboratives, and teams where multiple partners need to move together — especially when growth, complexity, or change makes the path feel unclear.

What challenges do you usually help with?

We're often brought in when priorities compete, coordination gets strained, or strategy needs to become shared action. Common focuses include clarifying direction, aligning leadership, improving how partners work together, and designing lightweight systems that keep progress moving.

How does an engagement typically work?

We start by understanding context, constraints, and relationships. Then we co-create actionable pathways — facilitation, planning, and practical supports — so decisions stay owned by your team and momentum is easier to maintain.

Do you support collaboratives and cross-sector partnerships?

Yes. We work often where sectors meet — helping groups build trust, shared language, and operating rhythms so partnerships can move from intent to coordinated execution.

How do we get started?

Usually with a conversation about what feels stuck, what outcomes matter, and what a sensible first step looks like. We'll be clear about scope, cadence, and how we'll collaborate before the work begins.

Franco Build isn’t your typical construction firm. They design and build custom housing solutions for large-scale EV batteries — from microgrid containers to climate-controlled battery vaults. It’s niche, complex, and deeply tied to the future of clean energy.
But their old site told none of that story. No mission. No clarity. Just concrete blocks of text and a handful of outdated renders. That’s where Evermind™ came in.

We started by listening. To how Franco talks about energy ethics, thermal envelopes, and site-specific modularity. And more importantly — how they wanted to be perceived.
Challenge
Franco Build, a pioneering firm in eco-architecture, faced significant challenges before partnering with us. Their previous branding efforts fell short, failing to convey the unique and innovative approach they took towards sustainable building practices. The brand's identity was devoid of the warmth and coherence that truly represented their mission. Internally, the team at Franco Build was well aware of the exceptional work they were doing, crafting structures that harmonized with nature and prioritized environmental responsibility.

However, this brilliance was not reflected in their external communications, leaving potential clients and the broader community unaware of the transformative impact of their projects. It was clear that a rebranding effort was essential to articulate their vision and values effectively, allowing the world to appreciate the beauty and significance of their eco-friendly designs.
This is who I’m speaking to.
This is who I’m speaking to.
Solution
In the beginning, we embarked on a series of in-depth strategy sessions, diving deep into the narratives that shaped their projects and the individuals behind them. This exploration allowed us to uncover the essence of their brand, leading us to the pivotal moment of rebranding. The new identity we crafted is one that exudes a sense of calm confidence, characterized by a palette of earthy tones that evoke a connection to nature. We chose geometric typography to convey modernity and clarity, ensuring that the visual elements resonate with their audience.

The modular Webflow site we developed is not only visually appealing but also equipped with a flexible content management system, allowing for easy updates and scalability. Every aspect of the design, from the tone of voice we established to the unique custom iconography we created, was meticulously crafted to embody Franco’s core belief in intentionality in building. This comprehensive approach ensures that every interaction with the brand reflects its values and mission, creating a cohesive experience for users.

What we learned together
Franco Build has taught us a valuable lesson about branding: the most powerful brands don’t have to raise their voices to be heard. Instead, they communicate with clarity and purpose, ensuring that their actions are aligned with their core values. This alignment is crucial because when a brand’s message resonates with its mission, everything else falls into place seamlessly.

The design becomes more cohesive, the outcomes are more successful, and the relationships built with customers and partners are stronger and more meaningful. It’s this kind of thoughtful and intentional work that we aspire to continue pursuing, as it not only enhances our brand but also fosters genuine connections with our audience.
